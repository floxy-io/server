Asimpleone-a-onesecureconnection

Floxy is a simple service that allow end-to-end ultra secure connection between a remote and a local machine. Zero configuration, no need to disable/configure firewall or change router settings, only one click and it's ready! In few steps, click on the button cook new floxy, and our service will create a new one binary with dual step async certificate and unique challenge to close a connection between your PC and a remote. You just can download one time each of one of binary remote and local.


>great for developers that has needs to expose a service or preview a feature to other peer.

>great for raspberry's owners that want to connect to ssh port over the internet without exposing it.


Comparing Floxy with VPN, it's really simpler, only few clicks and you have a end-to-end secure connection. Comparing it with Ngrok and other proxies services, Floxy don't expose remote machine over the internet, only who owns the local binary can connect to the remote. So, Let's start?

local>./floxyL -p 5555
remote>./floxyR -h localhost:22
