Asimpleend-to-endsecureconnection

Floxy is a simple service that allowend-to-end secure connection over the internet between a remote and a local machine. Zero configuration, no need to disable/configure firewall or change router settings, only one click and it's ready! In few steps, click on the button bellow, and our service will create an unique binary with fingerprint and async certificate to close a connection between your PC and a remote. 

local>./floxyL -p {{port}}
remote>./floxyR -h {{host}}:{{port}}

>great for developers that has needs to expose a service or preview a feature to other peer.

>great for raspberry's owners that want security to connect to ssh port over the internet.


Comparing Floxy with VPN, it's really simpler, only few clicks and you have a end-to-end secure connection. Comparing it with Ngrok and other proxies services, Floxy don't expose remote machine over the internet, only who owns the local binary can connect to the remote. So, Let's start?
